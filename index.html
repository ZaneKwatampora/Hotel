Facility information

Physical Facilities

Indoor Basketball Court:

Stay active with a game of basketball in our indoor court, perfect for both casual play and organized matches. The facility is equipped with professional-grade hoops and a spacious court for an enjoyable experience.


Gym:

Our state-of-the-art gym features modern equipment, including cardio machines, free weights, and strength training stations, ensuring a comprehensive workout experience for all fitness levels.


Outdoor Tennis Court:

Enjoy a game of tennis on our well-maintained outdoor court, featuring high-quality surfaces and equipment. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced player, our court provides the perfect setting for a match.

Relaxing Facilities

Arcade:

Step into our fun-filled arcade, featuring a variety of classic and modern games. From pinball to interactive video games, it's the perfect spot for guests of all ages to enjoy entertainment and friendly competition.


Spa:

Relax and unwind in our serene spa, offering a variety of rejuvenating treatments such as massages, facials, and body scrubs. Indulge in a tranquil atmosphere designed to refresh both body and mind.


Pool:

Dive into relaxation in our stunning outdoor pool, surrounded by lush landscaping and comfortable lounge areas. Whether you're looking to swim laps, soak up the sun, or simply unwind with a refreshing drink, our pool offers the perfect setting for relaxation and fun.
